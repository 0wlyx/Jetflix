Synopsis

Violet Evergarden is a woman who once joined the military. During the war, she was known as a cold-blooded soldier who only followed the orders of her superior, Major Gilbert. After the war ended, Violet was separated from the major who was declared missing after an explosion. Several years have passed since the end of the war that claimed many victims. The world is at peace and life is changing with the development of technology. Violet now works at the C.H. Postal Company after being invited by Gilbert's colleague, Major Hodgins, as an Auto-Memories Doll, or letter typist. Although the radio towers in Leidenschaftlich continue to be built and the telephone becomes more and more prevalent, Violet's fame as a letter writer never wanes. At the same time, Violet tries to move on with her life even though her heart still longs for Gilbert. Until one day, a mysterious letter is found, which opens up the hope that Gilbert might still be alive.
