Komi Can't Communicate

Komi, a high school Madonna, appears to be cold and above her classmates. However, in reality, her disposition is due to being unable to communicate with others. Tadano, Komi's classmate, is good at reading situations and quickly comes to realize that Komi has a communication disorder.
Romance

School Life

Series

Cast: Komi Shouko, Tadano Hitohito, Agari Himiko, Aizawa Tsukasa, Arai Kamiko, Karisu Maki

Synopsis

Hitohito Tadano is an ordinary boy who heads into his first day of high school with a clear plan: to avoid trouble and do his best to blend in with others. Unfortunately, he fails right away when he takes the seat beside the school's madonna—Shouko Komi. His peers now recognize him as someone to eliminate for a chance to sit next to the most beautiful girl in class. Gorgeous and graceful with long, dark hair, Komi is universally adored and immensely popular despite her mysterious persona. However, unbeknownst to everyone, she has crippling anxiety and a communication disorder which prevents her from wholeheartedly socializing with her classmates. When left alone in the classroom, a chain of events forces Komi to interact with Tadano through writing on the blackboard, as if in a one-way conversation. Being the first person to realize she cannot communicate properly, Tadano picks up the chalk and begins to write as well. He eventually discovers that Komi's goal is to make one hundred friends during her time in high school. To this end, he decides to lend her a helping hand, thus also becoming her first-ever friend.

Episodes

Episode 1 : It's just, I wish I could speak

24m

On their first day at Itan Private High School, Tadano meets Komi, a beautiful but quiet girl. While Komi becomes instantly popular, Tadano is disliked for sitting next to her. Tadano realizes that Komi suffers from severe social anxiety and suggests she communicate by writing on the chalkboard. They have their first conversation, and Komi reveals her dream of making 100 friends. Tadano promises to help her find the other 99. However, they soon discover that their school is filled with eccentric and unique individuals, making their task more challenging than expected.

Episode 2 : It's just a childhood friend. Plus More

24m

Tadano introduces Komi to Najimi, their childhood friend who has a habit of switching genders. Najimi reveals their past failed attempt to befriend Komi, causing them difficulty in interacting with her. While walking Komi home, Najimi is approached by a delinquent named Maa who confesses his love. Komi's speech impediment and stare make Maa think she's an assassin, and he runs away. Najimi decides to be Komi's friend for real. They start having lunch together, making Tadano even more unpopular. Najimi tries to help Komi overcome her social anxiety by sending her to order coffee alone, but she gets it wrong and cries. Tadano and Najimi apologize, finding Komi's sulking cute.

Episode 3 : It's just stage fright. Plus More

24m

Najimi advises Komi to befriend Himiko Agari, who also struggles with communication. Agari thanks Komi for scaring her, as it made it easier for her to interact with classmates. In gratitude, Agari becomes Komi's "lapdog." Komi buys her first phone and successfully asks for Najimi's number but feels awkward asking Agari due to her lapdog behavior. The same awkwardness occurs when she tries to ask Tadano for his number, with Agari accidentally ruining the moment. Komi is reluctantly elected class president but is instead voted class Goddess, with Tadano as president and Najimi as vice-president. Komi has an awkward first phone call with Tadano, which they both celebrate as an achievement. Komi is invited to play games with popular students, who let her win due to her popularity. Tadano sabotages the second game to spare Komi embarrassment, further adding to his unpopularity. However, Komi thanks him for helping her have fun.

Episode 4 : It's just a physical. Plus More

24m

During the physical exams, Makeru Yadano tries to beat Komi but fails, leading him to claim the exam is a draw. Tadano scores average on the exam. Ren Yamai, obsessed with Komi, kidnaps Tadano to keep him away from her. Najimi arranges for Komi and Najimi to visit Yamai's house, where they find Tadano in a closet. Yamai believes she would be a better friend to Komi but is rejected in favor of Tadano. Komi blames herself but asks Tadano if he will still be her friend. Yamai gives a confusing apology, and Komi decides to reveal her social anxiety to Yamai. Despite being shocked, Yamai finds Komi even cuter and vows to help her find 100 friends. Komi hesitates briefly but ultimately accepts Yamai as her friend.

Episode 5 : It's just my summer uniform. Plus More

24m

In the summer, Komi's attractiveness is even more apparent in her uniform. Yadano becomes determined to beat her in fitness tests but continuously loses. Komi's inability to shake his hand only fuels Yadano's determination to surpass her. Najimi asks to visit Komi's house, where they meet Komi's outgoing mother, Shūko. Najimi leaves Komi and Tadano alone, hoping for something to happen, but ends up disappointed. They stumble upon Komi's childhood photo album, and Najimi takes photos of their visit, which Komi keeps in a photo frame. Komi expresses her desire to visit a ramen shop, so Agari takes the group to Ramen Darou. Komi impresses everyone by flawlessly following the unspoken rules of eating ramen, earning admiration from her friends. A chūnibyō named Omoharu Nakanaka claims that Komi is Princess Komilia, her friend from a previous life. Tadano remembers his own chūnibyō phase in middle school. Recognizing Nakanaka's loneliness, Komi volunteers to be her gym partner. Najimi takes Tadano's umbrella and leaves a note, leading Komi and Tadano to share her umbrella. Tadano notices that Komi purposefully let his shoulder get wet to provide her with more cover. At a convenience store, Tadano realizes that Komi had read Najimi's note and waited for him so they could share her umbrella together.

Episode 6 : It's just a joke. Plus More

24m

Komi and Tadano struggle with telling jokes but still impress each other. Komi goes clothes shopping for the first time and ends up buying a dress chosen by Tadano. At the beauty salon, Komi's silence is misunderstood by Kamiko, but Maki understands her and gives her a haircut. Komi has trouble sleeping due to overthinking, but eventually falls asleep thinking about Tadano. Najimi asks for help studying, gets bored, and causes a library disturbance. Everyone passes their exams, angering Yadano that Komi scored higher. Over the summer, Komi and Tadano feel lonely but are too nervous to reach out. Najimi solves the problem by inviting them to go swimming at the pool.

Episode 7 : It's just the pool. Plus More

24m

Komi arrives at the pool wearing the dress Tadano chose, but Yamai forces her to buy a bikini. After tripping and scraping her knee, Komi sits alone until Tadano comforts her and accidentally holds her hand. They end the day with a water gun fight. Komi visits the library with her father, and they communicate non-verbally about her school life. Komi finds a book but is too scared to ask to borrow it. She cheers up a baby with her presence, observed by Tadano. Komi has fun alone at a park, and Tadano is awed by her beauty as he passes by.

Episode 8 : It's just Obon. Plus More

24m

Komi visits her relatives and bonds with her cousin Akira. During a conversation with her grandmother, Komi's nervous reaction hints at her feelings for Tadano. Komi and Tadano attend a summer festival with Najimi, where they have fun together. Komi manages to compliment Tadano verbally but erases her written confession. Komi, Najimi, and Yamai visit Tadano's home, where they reflect on the enjoyable summer. Komi tearfully admits she doesn't want the summer to end for the first time.

Episode 9 : It's just a country kid. Plus More

24m

Nokoko Inaka follows Komi to learn how to order food like a city girl. Nakanaka invites Komi to her house, and Tadano joins them to play video games. Najimi asks Komi and Tadano to help distribute tissues in a retail store. Komi becomes more confident throughout the day. Komi is too nervous to tell Tadano about a seed stuck on his face. She asks him to call her by her first name but fails, as does Najimi.

Episode 10 : It's just sports day. Plus More

24m

During the school's sports day, Komi meets the competitive Chika Netsuno and participates in a relay race. Despite slipping and falling, she receives encouragement from her friends and finishes in second place. Komi experiences jealousy when Nene Onemine helps Tadano with his duties as class president, but Onemine later befriends Komi and apologizes. Komi and her friends enjoy a trip to a photo booth and arcade, where Tadano wins a keychain for her. Komi treasures the keychain from Tadano.

Episode 11 : It's just a performance for the culture festival. Plus More

24m

Komi's class decides to do a maid café for the culture festival after hearing her suggestion. Onemine and Otori ask Komi for help in gathering materials, and during their time together, Komi opens up about her past insecurities and how happy she feels being useful. Komi, Najimi, and Tadano distribute flyers for the festival, and Komi even encounters the mother of a baby she previously cheered up. The girls surprise the boys with onigiri, and Komi is thrilled when Tadano picks the one she made. During the festival, Komi, Najimi, and Tadano dress as maids, and Komi finds Tadano's appearance adorable despite the boys feeling uncomfortable. Hitomi teases Tadano and takes a picture to show their mother.

Episode 12 : It's just the culture festival. Plus More

24m

At the festival, Najimi's shady tactics backfire, and they are forced to provide refunds. Komi, Tadano, and Najimi visit a haunted house and the rooftop confession area. Najimi is then dragged away by the meditation club, leaving Komi and Tadano to hang out with Onemine and Otori at the various stalls and activities. Komi's class is announced as winners of the festival, but due to Najimi's actions, they are not awarded the grand prize. At the dance party, Komi wishes to dance with Tadano, and they are joined by Najimi. After the festival, Komi's class goes to a karaoke bar where Komi receives praise for her singing despite being too nervous to actually perform. Tadano is motivated to sing when he sees Komi's support. Back at home, Komi cherishes the friendships she has made.
