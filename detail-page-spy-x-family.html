Spy x Family

The story follows a spy who must "build a family" to carry out a mission, unaware that the girl he adopts as a daughter and the woman he agrees to a fake marriage with are mind readers and assassins respectively.
Action
Comedy
Spy
Series

Cast: Yor Forger, Anya Forger, Loid Forger, Damian Desmond, Sylvia Sherwood, Franky Franklin

Synopsis

Corrupt politicians, frenzied nationalists, and other warmongering forces constantly jeopardize the thin veneer of peace between neighboring countries Ostania and Westalis. In spite of their plots, renowned spy and master of disguise "Twilight" fulfills dangerous missions one after another in the hope that no child will have to experience the horrors of war. In the bustling Ostanian city of Berlint, Twilight dons the alias of "Loid Forger," an esteemed psychiatrist. However, his true intention is to gather intelligence on prominent politician Donovan Desmond, who only appears rarely in public at his sons' school: the prestigious Eden Academy. Enlisting the help of unmarried city hall clerk Yor Briar to act as his wife and adopting the curious six-year-old orphan Anya as his daughter, Loid enacts his master plan. He will enroll Anya in Eden Academy, where Loid hopes she will excel and give him the opportunity to meet Donovan without arousing suspicion. Unfortunately for Loid, even a man of his talents has trouble playing the figure of a loving father and husband. And just like Loid is hiding his true identity, Yor—who is an underground assassin known as "Thorn Princess"—and Anya—an esper who can read people's minds—have no plans to disclose their own secrets either. Although this picture-perfect family is founded on deception, the Forgers gradually come to understand that the love they share for one another trumps all else.

Episodes

Episode 1 : Operation Strix

24m

Twilight, a Westalist spy, poses as a family member in Ostania to get close to leader Donovan Desmond. She adopted Anya, a telepathic daughter, who was later kidnapped, but Twilight rescued her. They find a new home, and Anya is accepted into Eden Academy. Twilight and Anya are informed of an interview with Anya's parents, possibly part of their larger plan.

Episode 2 : Secure a Wife

24m

Yor Briar, a suspected spy and assassin known as the "Thorn Princess," meets Loid by chance. Anya, who can read minds, discovers Yor's secret. Yor asks Loid to pretend to be her boyfriend at a party, but he accidentally claims to be her husband. They are chased by thugs, and Yor realizes Loid sees through her facade. They decide to marry for real, symbolically sealing their union with a grenade pin as a wedding ring, killing their pursuers.

Episode 3 : Prepare for the interview

24m

After getting married, Yor moved into Loid and Anya's friendly home. Loid tests them with fake interviews and they both fail. Loid takes them out to experience upper middle class life, such as going to the opera, museums, and attending political campaign events. Loid feels her plan is failing when Yor sees her stress, but they go to the park to relax. There, they witness a theft and Loid manages to catch the pickpocket. At night, Loid tests them again and they show progress in their responses.

Episode 4 : The Prestigious School's Interview

24m

On the day of the interview at Eden Academy, Loid was aware of the observation and assessment of the families present by Prefect Henry Henderson. He directed Yor and Anya to behave very gracefully according to Henderson's expectations. However, an unexpected event occurs when the school's farm animals break into the grounds and create chaos. Yor manages to stop the leader of the animal, while Anya uses her telepathic abilities to calm him down. They proceeded to interview the three prefects, including Henderson, and managed to answer the questions well. However, Prefect Murdoch Swan tried to derail them with a question that shocked Anya about the choice between her biological mother or her new mother, causing trauma. Loid decided to stop the interview and end the situation. After the interview, Henderson scolds Swan for her cruel question and gives appreciation to the Forger family. At home, Loid felt like a failure in the interview, but Yor and Anya assured her that the other Prefects would support them.

Episode 5 : Will They Pass or Fail

24m

The Forger family realized that Anya didn't pass the Eden Academy, but Henderson passed on the notice that Anya could have a chance to qualify if there was one applicant who didn't accept their pass. Three days later, Loid gets the news that Anya has been accepted into the school. Franky (Loid's informant) shows up at their apartment to celebrate until he and Yor get drunk. At Franky's insistence, Anya asked Loid to role-play a princess being helped by the main character in the Spy Wars cartoon Bondman in a palace. Loid then asked for help to rent a palace, inviting his fellow spy agents to "save" Anya from the kidnappers. After the role-play was over, Anya promised Loid that she would do well for him.

Episode 6 : The Friendship Scheme

24m

The Forger family went to the tailor shop to prepare school uniforms, while Loid and Sylvia Sherwood, known as "Handler," discussed the second phase of Operation Strix. They planned for Anya to gain Imperial Scholar status at Eden Academy by earning the Star of Stella. At the same time, Yor had to deal with a villain who wanted to kidnap Anya. Anya asks Yor to teach her martial arts techniques, and Yor agrees. On school orientation day, Loid secretly arranges for Anya to be in the same class as Damian Desmond, Donovan's son, in hopes of building a relationship through their children. During a school tour led by Henderson, Damian makes the situation worse, causing Anya to punch him in the face, acquire Tonitrus Bolts, and mess up two of Loid's plans.

Episode 7 : The Target's Second Son

24m

Anya and Becky encounter Damian and his friend on their first day at Eden Academy. Becky supports Anya and discourages her from apologizing to Damian. However, Loid, who secretly entered the school, sends a different message to Anya, urging her to apologize. Anya manages to apologize when Becky is not around. Damian, who had a crush on Anya, runs away and refuses her apology. Loid mistakenly believes his Plan B has failed. Back at home, Loid forces Anya to study to achieve an honor status, but she feels overwhelmed. Yor informs Loid that Anya sees him as a good father and asks him not to be harsh with her. Loid checks Anya's room and finds her asleep due to exhaustion from trying to study alone, and he moves her to her bed. Meanwhile, Yuri, Yor's sibling, learns about their sibling's marriage.

Episode 8 : The Counter-Secret Police Cover Operation

24m

Anya continues to struggle academically, causing Loid to doubt her ability to achieve the Stella Star within the four-month timeframe requested by the Handler. Yuri, who secretly works for the Ostanian State Security Service (SSS), visits Yor after interrogating a WISE informant working for the City Council. As Yuri is very close to Yor, they find it difficult to accept their sibling's marriage and bombard Loid with questions to reveal his identity. Frustrated, Yuri unintentionally reveals their SSS membership to Loid. In a drunken state, Yuri demands that Loid and Yor kiss after witnessing their strange behavior while touching hands, threatening to submit divorce papers to the City Hall if they fail to comply.

Episode 9 : Show Off How in Love You Are

24m

Unable to bear seeing Yor kiss another man, Yuri tries to stop them and accidentally receives a slap from the intoxicated and embarrassed Yor. Yuri flees the apartment with injuries. Meanwhile, when questioned by their superiors about their perception of Loid, Yuri refuses to accept him as Yor's spouse but acknowledges his good nature. Loid begins to doubt Yor's true motives after discovering Yuri's affiliation with the SSS. He secretly places a listening device in Yor's collar and overhears her discussing her failure as a wife with a colleague. While Yor is away from her office, Loid and Franky disguise themselves as SSS officers and begin interrogating her with false accusations. However, Yor's statements about Yuri and Loid convince Loid of his innocence. Loid then meets Yor on their way home and retrieves the listening device. He also advises Yor that she doesn't need to worry about trying to be the perfect wife as long as she is comfortable with herself. They reaffirm to each other to stay true to themselves, even as a fictional family.

Episode 10 : The Great Dodgeball Plan

24m

Anya and Damian receive news that they can earn the Stella Star by being crowned the Most Valuable Players in an upcoming dodgeball tournament. Consequently, Anya trains with Yor. At Eden Academy, Anya and Damian are placed on the same team. Anya reads Damian's mind and learns that he wants to win the Stella Star to step out of his sibling's shadow and gain recognition from his father. However, they have to face off against Bill Watkins, a physically imposing opponent who gradually eliminates members of their team. Anya manages to dodge Bill's throws by reading his mind. Frustrated, Bill hurls the ball with all his strength at Anya, but Damian shields her and gets eliminated from the team. Anya retrieves the ball and prepares to unleash a powerful throw she learned, but in the end, she throws it to the ground, which Bill then uses to eliminate her from the team. In the end, Henderson declares that no Stella Star will be awarded to the tournament winner.

Episode 11 : Stella

24m

After proving to be awkward in extracurricular activities, Anya is taken by Loid to participate in voluntary work at a hospital, as the Stella Star can also be obtained through community service. However, Anya's poor performance angers the staff there. Meanwhile, a boy named Ken is undergoing physical therapy and accidentally slips into a pool. Anya becomes aware of it and rushes to the pool to save him. Due to her lack of swimming skills, she nearly drowns, and Loid ultimately saves them both. Anya receives praise for being the only one to notice the incident and is awarded her first Stella Star. Loid then meets the Handler on a train, who pleads with him to contact Desmond immediately. At Eden Academy, Anya starts acting and takes pride in her Stella Star. Her classmates theorize that she cheated to obtain it. However, Damian defends Anya's integrity, which surprises everyone. Becky asks Anya what she wants as a gift. After Becky tells Anya about her dog, Anya asks Loid and Yor to adopt a dog at home, which they eventually agree to.

Episode 12 : Penguin Park

24m

After working tirelessly, Loid takes Anya and Yor to the Berlint Aquarium to convince his neighbors that they are a happy family. However, while there, he is forced to go on a mission to retrieve microfilm containing chemical weapons plans smuggled through a penguin. Anya secretly helps Loid find the penguin carrying the microfilm and tricks Yor into catching the bad guy. After both missions are accomplished, Loid gives Anya a stuffed penguin. The next day, Anya plays a spy mission with her toy. After Loid and Yor reprimand her for trying to enter their room, they are forced to play together to cheer Anya up.
